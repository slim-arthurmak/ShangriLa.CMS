Travel Preparation

Classified as tropical rainforest climate, Singapore stays hot and humid year-round, with no distinct seasons. Temperatures average around 31° C (88° F) during the day with little seasonal variation.

High40° C
Low30° C
wettestAugust

Currency

Singapore dollar (SGD)

Taxes

A 7% Goods and Services Tax (GST) and 10% service charge is applicable in most hotels and some private restaurants.

Tipping

Restaurants generally include a 10% service in the bill. If not, it is appropriate to tip the waiter 10% or whatever you feel is appropriate. However, tipping taxi drivers is not customary and is discouraged.

Dress

Given Singapore’s tropical climate, lightweight summer clothing is generally sufficient all year round. A suit is appropriate when conducting business. Some restaurants may require a jacket.

Visa

A visa is not required for most travellers from Commonwealth countries, the European Union and North America for stays of less than 90 days. Certain countries may require a visa to enter Singapore. Follow the link below to learn more.
